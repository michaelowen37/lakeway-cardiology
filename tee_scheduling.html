Transesophageal Echo (TEE):

Although this process will likely change in the near future, you can contact Michael Owen on Teams or Secure Chat to schedule.

Current process:
Michael will connect with Dr. Stoebner to create a new case request and TEE order then verify the availability of the doctor, patient, tech and OR. He then schedules from the order and blocks the AUHC Echo schedule to prevent double booking.

Important notes:
-The TEE order must go on the AUH Echo schedule so that the patient's PHI transfers to the ultrasound machine properly.

-Scheduling from an active order will not work so we must select "add on" from the active order in order to get an appointment onto the AUH Echo schedule.

-Once the order is added to the schedule (on the appropriate day), the start time must be edited.

-This is done by visiting the appointment desk and selecting "change appointment."

-By making this change, the time of the procedure will be accurately reflected on the sonographer's schedule.

-The AUHC Echo schedule must be blocked at the same time to prevent double booking.

-Typically, TEEs are done on Tuesdays around 12pm or 1pm depending on other cases.
